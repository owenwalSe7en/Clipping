## Platform Details

### 1. CONTENT REWARDS

**How it works:**
- Register clips with your bounty account
- Clips get assigned to bounty pools (varies by category/quality)
- Get paid when viewers watch your bounties
- Payment model: CPM-based (cost per thousand views)

**Payout structure:**
- Average: $2-5 per 1,000 views
- Minimum payout: $20 (before withdrawal)
- Payment frequency: Weekly or monthly (varies)
- Payment method: PayPal, bank transfer, crypto

**KYC requirements:**
- Tax ID (SSN in US, equivalent elsewhere)
- Government ID (driver's license, passport)
- Bank/PayPal account for payouts
- Verification time: 24-48 hours

**Best practices:**
- Submit clips early (higher chances of inclusion in bounty pools)
- Optimize titles with trending keywords
- Include proper descriptions (algorithms match viewer interests)
- Higher view clips = higher CPM
- Post diverse content types (algorithm diversifies your earnings)

**Gotchas:**
- Minimum $20 payout threshold (small earners wait longer to cash out)
- Clip approval can take 24-72 hours
- Some clips may be rejected (quality, content policy, duplicates)
- CPM varies based on clip quality/category (gaming = higher CPM)
- Payment can take 5-7 days after clearance

**2025-2026 status:** Stable, reliable, most consistent payouts. Good baseline platform.

### 2. WHOP

**How it works:**
- Create a creator profile on Whop marketplace
- Whop is a platform for creators to sell digital products (courses, access, exclusive content)
- Can earn via:
  1. **Bounty programs** (brands pay for content clips)
  2. **Creator marketplace** (sell exclusive clip packages to fans)
  3. **Affiliate** (promote other creators' content, earn commission)

**Payout structure:**
- Bounties: $1-10 per 1,000 views (varies by brand)
- Creator products: You set pricing (Whop takes 20-30% cut)
- Affiliate: 5-20% commission
- Minimum payout: $50
- Payment frequency: Monthly
- Payment method: Stripe, PayPal

**KYC requirements:**
- Tax ID (1099 form in US)
- Identity verification
- Bank account (direct deposit)
- Verification time: 24-48 hours

**Best practices:**
- Build a strong creator profile (bio, profile pic, sample clips)
- Diversify: Don't just rely on bounties
- Create "creator products" (e.g., "Top 10 Gaming Clips Pack" for $9.99)
- Use Whop's affiliate program to earn on other creators
- Engage with community (Whop values active creators)
- Post consistently (algorithm favors active creators)

**Gotchas:**
- Whop takes 20-30% cut from any products you sell (higher fees than others)
- Bounty rates can be lower than Content Rewards (varies by brand)
- Requires more setup than pure bounty platforms (need to create products, build audience)
- Competition higher (more creators on Whop)
- Payout threshold is $50 (higher than Content Rewards)

**2025-2026 status:** Growing. Whop is expanding bounty programs significantly. Good for creators willing to diversify beyond pure bounties.

### 3. VYRO

**How it works:**
- Video bounty platform focused on competitive creators
- Submit clips to Vyro bounty pools
- Vyro matches clips with brands/audiences
- Get paid based on views + engagement

**Payout structure:**
- Average: $2-8 per 1,000 views (higher end than Content Rewards in some categories)
- Minimum payout: $100 (high threshold)
- Payment frequency: Bi-weekly or monthly
- Payment method: Stripe, PayPal, crypto

**KYC requirements:**
- Tax ID
- ID verification
- Bank account
- Verification time: 48-72 hours

**Best practices:**
- Target underserved niches (Vyro pays more for unique content)
- High-quality clips perform better (Vyro has stricter quality standards)
- Gaming/streaming content performs best on Vyro (highest CPM)
- Consistency matters (Vyro algorithm favors regular uploaders)
- Engage with trending sounds/music (boosts discoverability)

**Gotchas:**
- **HIGH minimum payout threshold ($100)** - Need to earn $100 before first withdrawal
- Takes longer to get earnings (verification process can be slow)
- Quality standards are strict (more rejections than Content Rewards)
- Bounty pool competition is fierce (more creators chasing same bounties)
- Lower volume of available bounties vs. Content Rewards

**2025-2026 status:** Emerging, promising. Vyro is newer but growing fast. Good secondary platform, but requires more consistency to reach $100 minimum.

### 4. CLIPIFY (NEW - Discord-Based)

**How it works:**
- Operates entirely via Discord (no website)
- Community-driven bounty platform
- Creators submit clips to Discord channels
- Community members vote/rate clips
- Brands assign bounties to top-rated clips
- Get paid when viewers engage with your clips

**Payout structure:**
- Average: $3-10 per 1,000 views (highest potential)
- Minimum payout: $0 (can withdraw anytime, no threshold)
- Payment frequency: Weekly
- Payment method: PayPal, Stripe, crypto

**KYC requirements:**
- Flexible (less strict than traditional platforms)
- Basic Discord account
- Optional: Tax ID (only if earning > $600/year)
- Verification time: Instant (Discord-based, no formal KYC)

**Best practices:**
- Active in Discord community (vote on others' clips, comment, provide feedback)
- Engage with community = higher visibility/votes
- Post clips regularly (Discord algorithm favors consistency)
- Respond to feedback (community cares about creator engagement)
- Quality matters (community votes on quality, not just views)
- Trending/viral clips perform best (community gravitates to viral content)

**Gotchas:**
- **NO MINIMUM PAYOUT THRESHOLD** (good for new creators, can withdraw $5)
- Community-driven = less predictable (depends on community votes)
- Newer platform = smaller overall bounty pool (fewer brands currently)
- Discord-based = less professional/polished (good and bad)
- Growth potential = high, but current volume lower than others
- Tax documentation is DIY (you track, no 1099 from platform)

**2025-2026 status:** NEW & EMERGING. Clipify launched in 2024-2025. Low barrier to entry, high growth potential, but lower current bounty pool than established platforms.

## Optimal Publishing Strategy

### Week 1-2 (Validation)
1. Publish 10-15 clips to **all 4 platforms** simultaneously
2. Identify which platform gets most views (likely Content Rewards or Clipify)
3. Track payout rates per platform
4. Data: Which platform pays best for your content type?

### Week 3-4 (Optimization)
1. Prioritize platforms based on Week 1-2 data
2. Post to high-performing platform first (gets most views = higher CPM)
3. Then post to other platforms (secondary distribution)
4. A/B test: Different clip lengths, descriptions, hashtags per platform

### Month 2+ (Scaling)
1. Double down on best-performing platform
2. Use other platforms as secondary income
3. Diversify: Try trending content types on underperforming platforms
4. Monitor CPM rates weekly (platforms change rates seasonally)

## KYC Checklist

### Required Documents
- [ ] Government ID (driver's license, passport, national ID)
- [ ] Tax ID (SSN, EIN, ITIN, or equivalent)
- [ ] Bank account or PayPal for payouts
- [ ] Address verification (utility bill or government document)

### Timeline
- Content Rewards: 24-48 hours
- Whop: 24-48 hours
- Vyro: 48-72 hours
- Clipify: Instant (Discord-based)

### Tips
- Have all docs ready before signup (speeds up process)
- Use same legal name across all platforms (prevents payment issues)
- Keep PayPal/bank account info current (avoid payout delays)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ **Posting to only 1 platform** (diversify immediately)
2. ❌ **Waiting for KYC before posting** (KYC takes 24-72 hours, post while waiting)
3. ❌ **Same title/description across platforms** (each has different algorithm, tailor it)
4. ❌ **Not tracking which clips perform where** (you need this data by Week 2)
5. ❌ **Ignoring Clipify** (it's new, but has highest upside potential)
6. ❌ **Giving up after 10 clips** (need 50+ clips before trends emerge)
7. ❌ **Only chasing viral clips** (consistent 1K-10K view clips add up to more money than 1 viral clip)
