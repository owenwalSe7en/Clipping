# Video Clipping Tools: Comprehensive Feature Matrix

**Research Date:** June 2026
**Tools Analyzed:** OpusClip, vidyo.ai, Klap, Vizard, Munch, Eklipse, Framedrop, Powder, StreamLadder, Crossclip, Captions.ai

---

## Executive Summary

This research covers 11 video clipping and captioning tools, analyzing their AI capabilities, platform support, video quality, subtitle features, customization options, export capabilities, integrations, and watermark policies.

**Key Findings:**
- **Market Leaders:** OpusClip (10M+ users), Captions.ai (100K+ daily users)
- **Best Free Tier:** Vidyo.ai/Quso (75 credits/month with direct TikTok publishing)
- **Best for Streamers:** StreamLadder (free ClipGPT access, no watermarks)
- **Best for Gaming:** Framedrop and StreamLadder
- **Status Alert:** Powder discontinued late 2024/early 2025
- **Most Affordable Paid:** Crossclip ($4.99/month)
